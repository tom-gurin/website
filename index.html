Tom Gurin is a composer, multimedia artist, and carillonist. He holds a master's degree in music composition and computer music from the Haute école de musique de Genève and a bachelor's degree in composition from Yale University. He also holds diplomas from the École Normale de Musique de Paris and the Royal Carillon School in Belgium. He studied composition privately with Allain Gaussin in Paris.

He is a previous resident at the Cité internationale des arts in Paris, a Tremplin Leenaards / HEM Prize winner, a Mortimer Hays-Brandeis Traveling Fellowship recipient, a Fulbright Scholar, a Harriet Hale Woolley Scholar, and a Fellow of the Belgian-American Educational Foundation.
